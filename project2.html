Helium EVTOL Vehicle Development

Overview

Helium was a design team I founded with a group of friends at university. Our focus was on developing an EVTOL (Electric Vertical Takeoff and Landing) vehicle for the GoFly competition in California. This experience provided me with valuable education in design, leadership, and product management. I led the UX and cockpit design team, where we designed and developed the user controls, center console, and seating arrangements. My leadership in the manufacturing process enabled us to rapidly create a full-scale vehicle using composite layups and CNC tooling.

Technical Challenges

  • Timelines: Balancing schoolwork while working on this project was challenging but essential. Weekly planning was critical to building and testing effectively.
  • Cost / Budgets: We secured grants from our school and became highly efficient with planning to manage limited funding.
  • Space to Build: We used four different workspaces on campus, which encouraged spatial efficiency.
  • Build / Manufacturing: We learned composite layups, mold making, and CNC machining through hands-on assembly line work.

Methodologies & Tools

We used school-based design methods like component embodiments, personas, and simulations. Tools included Fusion 360, Excel/VBA, CNC machining, hand tools, composite layups, and 3D printing.

Solutions and Results

We overcame time constraints with transparent communication and weekly planning, completing manufacturing in two months. Grant funding allowed us to build multiple drone prototypes and fund team travel. By utilizing multiple build spaces and developing skills in composite manufacturing and CNC, we delivered a full-scale EVTOL prototype and gained mentorship-supported insights throughout.

Reflection

I learned how to manage a team of peers, build innovative technology, and lead manufacturing under tight constraints. This experience confirmed my passion for hands-on fabrication and inspired my goal of opening a fabrication shop to support design teams with technical builds.
